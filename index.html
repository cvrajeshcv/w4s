Areas We Excel

Marine & Offshore

The magnificent corrosion resistant property of FRP products are likely to be the ideal solution in marine industry. Wetro products like FRP Scrubber, FRP Pipes and Fittings, Ballast water treatment FRP tanks are widely used in offshore sector.

Oil & Gas

– FRP products Such as FRP Tanks and Pipelines plays an inevitable part in oil and gas sector. The non-corrosive characteristics boost its demand to almost all the nook and corners. Mainly FRP piping system is playing a vital role in efficient transportation across this field without any compromise in pressure, Temperature and Medium.

Energy

Wetro products are used extensively in energy sectors due to its durability, non- corrosive nature and ease of handling. Our products such as FRP Scrubbers, FRP Bioreactors, FRP Dampers, FRP Blowers, FRP Pipes and Fittings are providing solutions in many waste to energy projects efficiently.

Water and sewage

Wetro products such as FRP Dosing Tanks, Storage Tanks, Pipes and Fittings are widely used in Desalination / RO plants, Storm water, Drainage and sewerage treatment plants, Water storage and distribution units. Etc
